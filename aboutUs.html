About StudentHub

Why StudentHub?

Student: a person who pushes through hardship
Hub: the effective center of an activity, region, or network.

We wanted to create a fun and calm environment at the center of students' lives
We wanted to give back to a community that is often under-appreciated

Who is StudentHub?

We are a local-based student-run organization in Downtown Montreal.
We pride ourselves in elevating and showcasing our student communinty.
From the kitchen staff to the management team, everyone you meet is a student.

Our Mission

At StudentHub, we pride ourselves in our commitment to the planet.
We strive to minimize our carbon footprint by
Buying local produce
Offering plant-based options
Promoting sustainable choices
