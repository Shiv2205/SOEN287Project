COVID-19 measures

Washing Hands

It is required for customers to wash their hands as they enter the establishment.
Santizing stations are available at the entrance and at different places in the restaurant.

Wearing a Mask

It is required that staff and customers alike wear a mask.
* The mask must stay on at all times, even when talking

Santizing the Environment

Every 30 minutes and after a customer leaves, an employee sanitizes tables and seats.
